Jericoacoara, Brazil

Flat water, Beginner friendly Best season: July to January

Nestled in the northeast of Brazil, Jericoacoara (or simply "Jeri") is a kitesurfing paradise famous for its consistent winds and breathtaking lagoons. Once a small fishing village, it has evolved into a must-visit destination for kiters while maintaining its laid-back charm and environmental protection.

Wind conditions

Jericoacoara benefits from the reliable trade winds that blow from the east, providing consistent side-shore conditions with average speeds of 15-25 knots during the peak season. The wind typically picks up around mid-morning and maintains strength throughout the day, offering perfect conditions for riders of all levels.

Kitesurfing spots

  • Preá Beach: A 15km stretch of beach with side-onshore winds, perfect for beginners and freestyle riders.
  • Lago Paraíso: A beautiful flat-water lagoon with waist-deep water, ideal for learning and practicing tricks.
  • Guriú Lagoon: Another spectacular flat-water spot surrounded by dunes, offering butter-flat conditions.

Local tips

The village of Jericoacoara is situated within a national park, accessible only by 4x4 vehicles. Plan your transportation in advance, and don't miss the famous sunset at Duna do Pôr do Sol (Sunset Dune). The local cuisine features fresh seafood and açaí bowls, perfect for refueling after a day on the water.

Dakhla, Morocco

Flat water, Waves, All levels Best season: March to November

Located on a 40km peninsula in southern Morocco, Dakhla is a unique kitesurfing destination where the desert meets the ocean. This remote spot offers an extraordinary combination of flat-water lagoon and ocean wave riding, making it suitable for all disciplines and skill levels.

Wind conditions

Dakhla enjoys consistent trade winds throughout most of the year, with thermal enhancement creating reliable 15-30 knot conditions. The wind direction is predominantly northerly, providing cross-shore conditions at most spots. The best months are April to September when wind reliability exceeds 90%.

Kitesurfing spots

  • Dakhla Lagoon (La Bouche): The main spot with shallow, flat water and consistent wind, perfect for beginners and freestyle.
  • Point du Dragon: A wave spot with side-shore winds and waves reaching up to 2 meters, ideal for wave riding.
  • Speed Spot: A flat and shallow area inside the lagoon, perfect for practicing speed runs and freestyle tricks.

Local tips

Dakhla is remote and accommodation options are primarily kite camps and eco-lodges along the lagoon. The Moroccan-Saharan culture offers a unique experience, with delicious tagines and mint tea. Bring sun protection as the desert sun can be intense, and a wetsuit (3/2mm) is recommended even in summer as water temperatures can be cooler than expected.
