Frequently Asked Questions

What is the deposit amount?

A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due 30 days before the trip start date.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 60+ days before the trip start date receive a full refund minus a €50 processing fee. Cancellations 30-60 days prior receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 30 days prior are non-refundable.

Do you offer insurance?

We recommend all participants obtain travel insurance that covers adventure sports. We can provide recommendations upon request.

What equipment do I need to bring?

A detailed packing list will be provided after booking. Kite equipment rental is available if needed.

Can I book a private trip?

Yes, we offer private customized trips. Please select "Custom Adventure" in the trip selection and provide details in the special requests field.
